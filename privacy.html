Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Disclaimer: This app uses AI-generated image transformations for entertainment purposes only. The generated images may appear realistic or misleading. Users are solely responsible for how they use, share, or distribute generated content. Please use the app responsibly and respect the privacy and consent of others.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Photos you upload: Images you provide for AI processing
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for authentication
  • Usage data: How you interact with the App, features used, and preferences
  • Purchase information: Transaction history for subscriptions and credits

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your photos and generate AI images
  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process transactions and manage your account
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about updates and support

3. Photo Processing

When you upload a photo:

  • Photos are sent to our secure servers for AI processing
  • Photos are processed by our AI service providers
  • We do not permanently store your original photos
  • Generated images are delivered to your device and may be temporarily cached

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • AI Service Providers: To process and generate images
  • Payment Processors: To handle subscriptions via Apple's App Store
  • Analytics Services: To understand App usage (anonymized data only)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication using Apple's App Attest
  • Regular security audits and updates

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support team.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us through the App's support feature.
