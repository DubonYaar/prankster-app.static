What is Prankster?

Prankster is an iOS app built for anyone who wants to spice up a birthday with creative, good-natured humor. Pick a photo, let the AI work its magic, and get high-quality images you can share with friends and family.

Whether you are planning a surprise or just want a memorable card, Prankster keeps the focus on fun—without taking itself too seriously.
