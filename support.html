Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?
Each image generation uses one credit. New users receive free credits to get started. You can purchase more credits or subscribe for additional credits each month.
Why didn't my image generate correctly?
AI image generation works best with clear, well-lit photos where faces are clearly visible. Try using a different photo or adjusting the settings for better results.
How do I restore my purchases?
Go to the subscription settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime through your Apple ID settings. Go to Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions on your device to manage your subscription.
Is my data safe?
Yes, we take privacy seriously. Photos are processed securely and not permanently stored. Please read our Privacy Policy for more details.
What happens to unused credits?
Subscription credits refresh each billing period. Bonus credits (from sign-up) remain available until used.
